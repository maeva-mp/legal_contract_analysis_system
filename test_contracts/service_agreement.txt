Service Agreement – IT Support Services

This Service Agreement is made between NetSecure Ltd and OceanView Hotels. The provider shall deliver IT support services from 1 April 2023 to 31 March 2024.

The client shall pay a monthly fee of MUR 25,000. Payment must be made by the 10th of each month. The provider agrees to process personal data in accordance with the Data Protection Act 2017 and obtain consent from data subjects.

This agreement is governed by Article 1108 of the Civil Code, requiring consent, capacity, object, and cause. Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days’ notice.

Any amendment must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
