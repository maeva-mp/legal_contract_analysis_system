Employment Agreement – Junior Software Developer

This Employment Contract is made between CodeCraft Ltd and Jane Doe. The employee shall commence work on 1st February 2023. The employment contract includes written particulars as required under the Workers’ Rights Act 2019. The employee must submit a resignation notice at least 14 days in advance.

The employer shall pay a monthly salary of MUR 45,000. Payment shall be made by the 5th of each month. The employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information.

Termination without cause may occur with 30 days’ notice. Any dispute shall be resolved through arbitration. The employer shall not be liable for indirect damages.
